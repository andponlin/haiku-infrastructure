HTTP 403 - Forbidden - UK

Unfortunately, we are no longer able to serve collaborative community services to users connecting directly from the United Kingdom as of March 16, 2025.

Haiku, Inc. is not prepared, nor willing to use substantial financial funds donated from users globally to navigate the complex legal framework of the UK's Online Safety Act at this time.

A broader overview of the UK OSA is available here.

The UK Ofcom has published an 84-page 'brief summary' of OSA requirements here.

Non user-to-user online services (such as the website, software repositories, cgit, etc) will not be impacted by this block.
